MLB Player

The career that I chose was a Major League Baseball player.

Education

Becoming a professional baseball player to not have any academic requirements. In fact, many get drafted out of highschool.

Training

There are no training requirements for mlb players. Excellent performance in the sport is usually the most significant requirement. The only other way to train is by practicing

Salary

The average salary for a major league baseball player is about 4.9 million US dollars as of 2023.

Technology

Baseball uses technology like radar systems and cameras to monitor and analyze every pitch made. It provides key data on pitch velocity, trajectory, and spin.
